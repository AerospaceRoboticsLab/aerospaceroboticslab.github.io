### Join the lab

We welcome motivated students interested in autonomy, control, and robotics for extreme environments.

**Prospective PhD students:** apply to the Daniel Guggenheim School of Aerospace Engineering and mention your interest in the Aerospace Robotics Lab.

**Current GT students:** reach out to Dr. Nakka with your CV and a short note on your research interests.
