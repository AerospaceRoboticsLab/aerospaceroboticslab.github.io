## Courses

**AE 4321 — Space Systems Design I**
First semester of the space systems capstone design sequence, covering mission formulation, requirements, and preliminary spacecraft design. *Offered: Fall 2024, Fall 2025.*

**AE 4322 — Space Systems Design II**
Second semester of the capstone sequence, advancing from preliminary design to detailed design, analysis, and integration of a complete space system. *Offered: Spring 2025.*

Full course descriptions are available in the [Georgia Tech Course Catalog](https://catalog.gatech.edu/).
