# Aerospace Robotics Lab

Welcome to the **Aerospace Robotics Lab** at **Georgia Tech**. We enable aerospace systems to operate autonomously — safely, intelligently, and collaboratively — in uncertain and extreme environments, whether on the Moon, in orbit, or beyond.

At the heart of our work is a fundamental question: **how can robotic systems make intelligent decisions in the face of uncertainty, while ensuring safety, robustness, and mission success?** We formulate and solve information-cost stochastic optimal control problems, drawing on nonlinear control, distributional robustness, convex optimization, and probability theory.
