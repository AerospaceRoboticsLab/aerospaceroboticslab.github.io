Dr. Nakka directs the Aerospace Robotics Lab and is an Assistant Professor in the Daniel Guggenheim School of Aerospace Engineering at Georgia Tech. His research develops autonomy for aerospace systems operating in uncertain and extreme environments — on the Moon, in orbit, and beyond. Before joining Georgia Tech, he was a Robotics Technologist at NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory, where he worked on CADRE, EELS, and DARPA LINC.
