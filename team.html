A group of researchers working across nonlinear control, autonomy, and robotics — from theory to hardware in the field.

Director / Principal Investigator

Dr. Yashwanth Nakka

Assistant Professor, PhD · Daniel Guggenheim School of Aerospace Engineering · Georgia Institute of Technology

Dr. Nakka directs the Aerospace Robotics Lab, developing autonomy for space systems operating in uncertain and extreme environments — on the Moon, in orbit, and beyond.
